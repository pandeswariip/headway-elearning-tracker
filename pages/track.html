About Headway Tracker

Headway Tracking is an all-in-one training & tracking platform that perfectly works as training tracking software with its comprehensive analytics suite.
In this feature, you’ll be able to monitor various courses. That includes all sorts of completion data, performance data, registration and login data, and other useful data.

Key features:

  • Course completion by user
  • Rapid refresh quiz reports
  • Actionable reports
  • Custom reports
  • Push notifications

Headway gives you access to three different kinds of reports: Reminder, Progress chart, and rapid refresh quiz reports. On the Progress chart, you can monitor the learning progress of your course within enrolled course. Course completion by user is where you’ll find a more detailed learning progress report for each individual. And lastly, rapid refresh quiz reports are where you’ll access quiz results data.
Not only that, but you also have flexibility in how you want to manage your data. Headway reports are actionable—meaning, from the data you see in the dashboard, you can instantly take action and complete your courses. Overall, these features can help you monitor the learning progress, on all course platform.
